About McDonald Farms

McDonald Farms is a family-run visitor attraction nestled in the beautiful countryside between Edinburgh and Carlisle. Our mission is to provide a fun, relaxing, and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

Our Story

Originally a small farm, McDonald Farms has grown into a vibrant destination featuring a petting zoo, a cozy café, a farm shop with local produce, an exciting play park, and scenic walking trails. We take pride in preserving the charm of rural life while welcoming visitors from near and far.
