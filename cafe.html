Relax at Our Cozy Café

Take a break and enjoy a delicious selection of homemade cakes, light lunches, and freshly brewed coffee at McDonald Farms’ Café. Whether you're here for a quick snack or a leisurely meal, our café has something for everyone.

Menu Highlights:

Homemade Cakes: Freshly baked daily, from chocolate cake to tangy lemon drizzle.
Fresh Coffee: Enjoy a cup of locally roasted coffee or a soothing tea.
Light Lunches: Delicious sandwiches, soups, and salads made with local ingredients.
Specialty Treats: Seasonal pastries and kid-friendly snacks.

With indoor and outdoor seating, our café is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the fresh countryside air.

Location: Between Edinburgh and Carlisle | Open Mon-Sat: 9AM-6PM, Sun: 10AM-4PM
