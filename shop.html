Discover Our Farm Shop & Garden Centre

Explore the best of local produce and gardening essentials at McDonald Farms’ Farm Shop & Garden Centre. Whether you’re looking for fresh food, unique crafts, or beautiful plants, we have something for everyone.

What We Offer:

Locally Sourced Produce: Fresh fruits, vegetables, and dairy products straight from our farm and neighbouring farms.
Handmade Crafts: Unique handmade goods, including candles and pottery.
Plants & Gardening Supplies: A wide variety of plants, flowers, and tools to help your garden flourish.
Homemade vegan soap: A vegan soap using local plants and flowers.

Take home a piece of McDonald Farms and support local businesses by shopping with us.

Location: Between Edinburgh and Carlisle | Open Mon-Sat: 9AM-6PM, Sun: 10AM-4PM
