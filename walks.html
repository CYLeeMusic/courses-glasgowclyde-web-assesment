Explore Our Scenic Walks

Take a peaceful stroll through the McDonald Farms countryside on one of our scenic walking trails. Whether you're looking for a short, relaxing walk or a longer hike, we have trails to suit every level.

Walking Trails:

Farm Trails: Wander through the heart of the farm, enjoy the fresh air, and observe farm animals from a distance.
Woodland Walks: Immerse yourself in nature and explore the beautiful wooded areas surrounding the farm.
Countryside Views: Enjoy panoramic views of the rolling hills, fields, and woodlands of the McDonald Farms estate.
Up to the hills Hiking through the hills that you can see the amazing scenary nearby.

Walking through our scenic trails offers the perfect opportunity to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of everyday life while taking in the serene beauty of the countryside.

Location: Between Edinburgh and Carlisle | Open Mon-Sat: 9AM-6PM, Sun: 10AM-4PM
