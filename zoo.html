Meet Our Friendly Animals!

Come and meet our friendly farm animals at McDonald Farms’ Petting Zoo! Our zoo is home to a variety of adorable creatures, including:

Goats: Playful and curious, they love to interact with visitors.
Alpacas: Gentle and fluffy, these charming animals are a favorite.
Rabbits: Soft and cuddly, perfect for a hands-on experience.
Chickens & Ducks: Watch them roam freely and splash around.

The Petting Zoo is designed to be safe and interactive, allowing children and adults alike to learn about farm life and enjoy the company of our animals.

Location: Between Edinburgh and Carlisle | Open Mon-Sat: 9AM-6PM, Sun: 10AM-4PM
